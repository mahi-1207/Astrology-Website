Capricorn ♑

Disciplined, ambitious, and practical

Capricorn Overview

Capricorn, the tenth sign of the zodiac, is ruled by Saturn and symbolized by the Goat. People born under this sign (December 22 - January 19) are known for their determination, responsibility, and strong work ethic. They are highly goal-oriented and value stability and discipline in life.

Capricorn Traits

  • Element: Earth
  • Ruling Planet: Saturn
  • Symbol: Goat ♑
  • Strengths: Responsible, disciplined, determined, practical
  • Weaknesses: Pessimistic, rigid, overly serious
  • Compatible Signs: Taurus, Virgo, Scorpio

Love & Relationships

Capricorn individuals seek stable and long-term relationships. They are deeply loyal and committed partners who value trust and emotional security. They may take time to open up but are incredibly devoted once they do.

Career & Finance

Capricorns thrive in careers that require responsibility, leadership, and strategic planning. They excel in business, finance, law, and engineering. They are disciplined with their finances and prioritize long-term stability and success.

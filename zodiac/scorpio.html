Scorpio ♏

Passionate, determined, and resourceful

Scorpio Overview

Scorpio, the eighth sign of the zodiac, is ruled by Pluto and Mars and symbolized by the Scorpion. People born under this sign (October 23 - November 21) are known for their intensity, emotional depth, and ability to transform and reinvent themselves. They are natural leaders and highly intuitive individuals.

Scorpio Traits

  • Element: Water
  • Ruling Planet: Pluto & Mars
  • Symbol: Scorpion ♏
  • Strengths: Brave, loyal, strategic, ambitious
  • Weaknesses: Jealous, secretive, possessive
  • Compatible Signs: Cancer, Pisces, Virgo

Love & Relationships

Scorpio individuals are deeply passionate and seek intense, transformative relationships. They are extremely loyal but expect the same in return. They value deep emotional connections and trust above all else.

Career & Finance

Scorpios excel in careers that require strategy, determination, and resilience. They thrive in roles such as research, psychology, law enforcement, and finance. They are highly resourceful with money and prefer financial security and control.

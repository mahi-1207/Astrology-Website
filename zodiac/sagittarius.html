Sagittarius ♐

Adventurous, optimistic, and independent

Sagittarius Overview

Sagittarius, the ninth sign of the zodiac, is ruled by Jupiter and symbolized by the Archer. People born under this sign (November 22 - December 21) are known for their love of travel, philosophical nature, and enthusiastic outlook on life. They crave adventure and freedom in everything they do.

Sagittarius Traits

  • Element: Fire
  • Ruling Planet: Jupiter
  • Symbol: Archer ♐
  • Strengths: Optimistic, adventurous, intellectual, honest
  • Weaknesses: Impatient, blunt, overconfident
  • Compatible Signs: Aries, Leo, Aquarius

Love & Relationships

Sagittarius individuals are adventurous and fun-loving partners who seek excitement in relationships. They value honesty and freedom, and they need a partner who shares their thirst for knowledge and travel.

Career & Finance

Sagittarius natives thrive in careers that allow them to explore new ideas and places. They excel in roles such as teaching, writing, philosophy, and travel-based industries. Financially, they are generous but need to ensure they plan for the future.

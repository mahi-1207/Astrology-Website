Cancer ♋

Emotional, intuitive, and nurturing

Cancer Overview

Cancer, the fourth sign of the zodiac, is ruled by the Moon and symbolized by the Crab. People born under this sign (June 21 - July 22) are deeply intuitive, sensitive, and emotional. They cherish home, family, and close relationships. Despite their tough exterior, they have a deeply caring and protective nature.

Cancer Traits

  • Element: Water
  • Ruling Planet: Moon
  • Symbol: Crab ♋
  • Strengths: Loyal, protective, caring, intuitive
  • Weaknesses: Moody, overly sensitive, defensive
  • Compatible Signs: Scorpio, Pisces, Taurus

Love & Relationships

Cancer individuals are deeply romantic and seek emotional security in relationships. They are nurturing partners who give their all to the people they love. However, they require reassurance and trust to truly open up.

Career & Finance

Cancer natives excel in careers that require empathy, creativity, and leadership. They thrive in fields such as healthcare, psychology, teaching, and culinary arts. Their financial habits are careful, as they prioritize security and stability.

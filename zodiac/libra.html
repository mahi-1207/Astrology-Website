Libra ♎

Charming, diplomatic, and balanced

Libra Overview

Libra, the seventh sign of the zodiac, is ruled by Venus and symbolized by the Scales. People born under this sign (September 23 - October 22) are known for their love of harmony, fairness, and social grace. They excel in diplomacy and have a strong sense of justice.

Libra Traits

  • Element: Air
  • Ruling Planet: Venus
  • Symbol: Scales ♎
  • Strengths: Diplomatic, social, fair-minded, charming
  • Weaknesses: Indecisive, avoids confrontation, self-indulgent
  • Compatible Signs: Gemini, Aquarius, Leo

Love & Relationships

Libra individuals are romantic and seek balanced, harmonious relationships. They value deep conversations and emotional connections. They are affectionate partners who enjoy giving and receiving love in equal measure.

Career & Finance

Libras thrive in careers that require negotiation, creativity, and people skills. They excel in law, design, diplomacy, and entertainment. Financially, they enjoy luxury but need to balance spending with saving for stability.

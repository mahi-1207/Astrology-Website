Pisces ♓

Empathetic, artistic, and dreamy

Pisces Overview

Pisces, the twelfth sign of the zodiac, is ruled by Neptune and symbolized by the Fish. People born under this sign (February 19 - March 20) are known for their compassion, creativity, and intuitive nature. They have a deep connection to emotions and spirituality, often thriving in artistic and healing professions.

Pisces Traits

  • Element: Water
  • Ruling Planet: Neptune
  • Symbol: Fish ♓
  • Strengths: Compassionate, artistic, intuitive, gentle
  • Weaknesses: Overly emotional, escapist, idealistic
  • Compatible Signs: Cancer, Scorpio, Taurus

Love & Relationships

Pisces individuals are deeply romantic and seek soulful connections. They are caring and empathetic partners who thrive in relationships built on emotional depth, trust, and understanding.

Career & Finance

Pisces thrive in careers that allow them to express their creativity and compassion. They excel in roles such as artists, musicians, counselors, and healers. Financially, they need to focus on practical management to avoid excessive spending.

Taurus ♉

Reliable, patient, and determined

Taurus Overview

Taurus, the second sign of the zodiac, is ruled by Venus and symbolized by the Bull. People born under this sign (April 20 - May 20) are known for their determination, loyalty, and appreciation for the finer things in life. They value stability and consistency in all aspects of their lives.

Taurus Traits

  • Element: Earth
  • Ruling Planet: Venus
  • Symbol: Bull ♉
  • Strengths: Reliable, patient, practical, devoted
  • Weaknesses: Stubborn, possessive, uncompromising
  • Compatible Signs: Virgo, Capricorn, Cancer

Love & Relationships

Taurus individuals are deeply romantic and seek long-term, committed relationships. They are devoted and affectionate partners who value trust and stability in love.

Career & Finance

Taurus thrives in careers that offer stability and security. They excel in fields such as finance, art, real estate, and agriculture. They are financially responsible and prefer long-term investments over risky ventures.

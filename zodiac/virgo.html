Virgo ♍

Practical, analytical, and reliable

Virgo Overview

Virgo, the sixth sign of the zodiac, is ruled by Mercury and symbolized by the Maiden. People born under this sign (August 23 - September 22) are known for their meticulous attention to detail, deep sense of humanity, and hardworking nature. They thrive in structured environments and enjoy helping others.

Virgo Traits

  • Element: Earth
  • Ruling Planet: Mercury
  • Symbol: Maiden ♍
  • Strengths: Organized, practical, diligent, analytical
  • Weaknesses: Overly critical, perfectionist, reserved
  • Compatible Signs: Taurus, Capricorn, Cancer

Love & Relationships

Virgo individuals value stability and loyalty in relationships. They may take time to open up emotionally, but once they do, they are incredibly devoted and caring partners. They seek a partner who appreciates their dedication and meticulous nature.

Career & Finance

Virgos excel in careers that require organization, analysis, and problem-solving. They thrive in roles such as healthcare, finance, writing, and research. They are financially cautious and prefer planning ahead to ensure security.

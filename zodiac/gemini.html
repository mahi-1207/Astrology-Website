Gemini ♊

Adaptable, curious, and intellectual

Gemini Overview

Gemini, the third sign of the zodiac, is ruled by Mercury and symbolized by the Twins. People born under this sign (May 21 - June 20) are known for their intelligence, adaptability, and lively personalities. They are naturally curious and love engaging in new experiences and conversations.

Gemini Traits

  • Element: Air
  • Ruling Planet: Mercury
  • Symbol: Twins ♊
  • Strengths: Adaptable, outgoing, intelligent, witty
  • Weaknesses: Inconsistent, indecisive, easily distracted
  • Compatible Signs: Libra, Aquarius, Aries

Love & Relationships

Gemini individuals are fun-loving and sociable partners who seek excitement in relationships. They are highly communicative and enjoy deep, meaningful conversations. However, they need variety and mental stimulation to keep their interest alive.

Career & Finance

Gemini natives thrive in careers that allow them to use their intellect and communication skills. They excel in journalism, marketing, public relations, and technology. They are flexible with finances but may need to focus on long-term stability.

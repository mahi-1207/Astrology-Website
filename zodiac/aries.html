Aries ♈

Bold, ambitious, and energetic

Aries Overview

Aries, the first sign of the zodiac, is ruled by Mars and symbolized by the Ram. People born under this sign (March 21 - April 19) are natural-born leaders, full of passion and determination. They are known for their competitive spirit and enthusiasm for life.

Aries Traits

  • Element: Fire
  • Ruling Planet: Mars
  • Symbol: Ram ♈
  • Strengths: Courageous, determined, confident, enthusiastic
  • Weaknesses: Impulsive, impatient, aggressive
  • Compatible Signs: Leo, Sagittarius, Gemini

Love & Relationships

Aries individuals are passionate lovers who seek excitement in relationships. They are fiercely loyal and protective of their loved ones but require a partner who can match their energy and enthusiasm.

Career & Finance

Aries thrive in careers that allow them to take initiative and showcase their leadership skills. They excel in fields such as entrepreneurship, sports, law enforcement, and sales. Financially, they are ambitious but need to manage impulsive spending habits.
